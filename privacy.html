1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Resistance LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our mobile applications (collectively, the "Apps") available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using our Apps.

By downloading, installing, or using any of our Apps, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use our Apps.

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications published by Resistance LLC on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. It does not apply to any third-party websites, services, or applications that may be linked to or otherwise accessible from our Apps.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

Our Apps are designed with user privacy as a core principle. Specifically, we do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (such as name, email address, phone number, or postal address)
  • Account credentials or authentication data
  • Financial information or payment data
  • Location data (precise or approximate)
  • Device contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history or search history
  • User-generated content
  • Communications or messages
  • Biometric data

All data created or processed by our Apps (including any user preferences, settings, or app-generated content) is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party, except as described in Section 4 below.

4. Analytics Information — Google Analytics for Firebase

We use Google Analytics for Firebase ("Google Analytics"), a web analytics service provided by Google LLC ("Google"), to collect anonymous usage data about how users interact with our Apps. This information helps us understand app performance, identify areas for improvement, and enhance the overall user experience.

4.1 Information Collected by Google Analytics

Google Analytics automatically collects certain non-personally-identifiable information, which may include:

  • App session data (session duration, frequency of use, and screens viewed)
  • Feature interaction events (which features or screens are accessed and in what sequence)
  • General device information (device type, operating system version, and app version)
  • Approximate geographic region (country or region level, derived from IP address)
  • App performance data (crash reports and error logs)

Google Analytics does not, and is not configured by us to, collect your name, email address, or any other directly personally identifiable information. All data transmitted to Google Analytics is aggregated and anonymized to the extent technically feasible.

4.2 Data Minimization and Anonymization

We have configured Google Analytics with the following privacy-protective settings:

  • IP address anonymization is enabled, meaning your full IP address is never logged by Google Analytics
  • We do not enable advertising features, remarketing, or interest-based advertising within Google Analytics
  • We do not link Google Analytics data to any personally identifiable information

4.3 Google's Use of Analytics Data

Data collected through Google Analytics is transmitted to and stored on Google's servers. Google's use of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. Google may use this data to improve its own products and services in accordance with its privacy policy.

We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy to understand how Google handles data collected through its analytics services.

4.4 Opting Out of Google Analytics

You may limit the collection of data by Google Analytics through the following methods:

  • On Android devices: Navigate to Google Settings > Ads and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization."
  • On iOS devices: Navigate to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking and disable tracking for our Apps, or enable "Limit Ad Tracking."
  • You may also reset your device's advertising identifier at any time through your device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed to, designed for, or intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has used our Apps, please note that our Apps do not collect personal information from any user.

Our use of Google Analytics is configured in compliance with applicable laws and platform policies regarding children's privacy, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act ("COPPA").

6. Data Security

Because our Apps do not collect or transmit personal information (other than the anonymized analytics data described in Section 4), the privacy risks associated with using our Apps are minimal. The analytics data transmitted to Google is protected by Google's industry-standard security infrastructure.

Any data stored locally on your device (such as app preferences and settings) is protected by your device's built-in security mechanisms, including device encryption and access controls. We recommend that you maintain appropriate security protections on your device, such as using a strong passcode or biometric authentication.

7. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data on our own servers, as no such data is collected. Google Analytics data is retained by Google in accordance with Google's data retention policies. Locally stored app data (such as settings and preferences) remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its data through your device settings.
